# About Us

**Transforming Data into Insightful Action**

Welcome to the official GitHub repository for **Infera Group LLC**, a data science and analytics consultancy founded by James Eells, PhD. We specialize in applying predictive modeling, econometrics, and data visualization to solve complex business problems and drive measurable impact.

## 👋 About James Eells

I'm a data science leader with over two decades of experience in financial services, higher education, and social impact domains. My work blends rigorous statistical modeling with practical business insight to help organizations make smarter, faster decisions.

I hold a PhD in Economics from Oklahoma State University and have led high-impact analytics initiatives at Ameriprise Financial, American Express, and Oklahoma State University. My approach emphasizes transparency, reproducibility, and alignment with stakeholder goals.

## 🔍 Services

At Infera Group, we help clients:

- Design and validate predictive models
- Measure program impact using causal inference
- Optimize marketing and service strategies
- Build interactive dashboards and data pipelines
- Modernize analytics ecosystems (e.g., cloud migration, MLOps)
