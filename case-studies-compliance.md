# ⚖️ Compliance & Governance Case Studies  
Aligning analytics with risk, regulation, and responsible oversight.

---

## 🔍 Predictive Risk Modeling for Advisor Surveillance

**Client Context**  
Rule-based surveillance created high false positives and consumed analyst bandwidth.

**Approach**  
Modeled five risk behaviors using logistic regression. Created a General Risk Index based on probability × impact.

**Impact**  
Reduced false positives by 15%. Improved targeting and introduced a transparent, data-driven compliance layer.

---

## ☁️ Migrating Legacy Analytics to AWS Cloud

**Client Context**  
SAS-based ecosystem was unscalable and non-compliant with modern ML practices.

**Approach**  
Migrated datasets via Glue/Athena. Rebuilt models using SageMaker AutoML and scored through Batch Transform. Integrated fairness with SageMaker Clarify.

**Impact**  
Boosted scoring performance by 30%. Delivered modern MLOps compliance ahead of schedule and reduced operational costs.

## ✅ Achieving SR 11-7 Compliance for Model Governance

**Client Context**  
Federal Reserve review flagged non-compliance with SR 11-7 across model lifecycle.

**Approach**  
Led a 12-month remediation program with gated milestones, collaborative remediation, and embedded lifecycle uplift.

**Impact**  
Achieved full compliance on schedule. Raised governance maturity and restored regulator confidence.
