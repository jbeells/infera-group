## 🎓 OSU Entry-Level Placement Assessment (ELPA)

**Client Context**  
Traditional placement methods at OSU relied heavily on standardized tests, often resulting in under-placement and excessive remediation.

**Approach**  
Developed a regression-based system—the Predicted Grade Index (PGI)—to estimate course success probabilities using GPA, class rank, and ACT/SAT scores. Embedded into enrollment workflows and recalibrated with institutional research.

**Impact**  
Significantly improved placement accuracy for 4,400+ students annually. Reduced remediation to just 4.86% and expanded placement support for students without test scores.

## 🧪 Accelerating Marketing Insights with Multivariable Testing

**Client Context**  
Legacy A/B testing missed interactions between creative elements in mail campaigns.

**Approach**  
Implemented Plackett-Burman and fractional factorial designs to capture main effects and two-factor interactions.

**Impact**  
Reduced test cells needed, improved campaign performance, and established multivariable testing as the new creative standard.
