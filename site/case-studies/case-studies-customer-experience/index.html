    🤝 Customer Experience Case Studies

    Enriching service delivery through intelligent prioritization and engagement.

    ⚖️ Real-Time Optimization of Dispute Resolution

    Client Context
    Manual, high-cost billing disputes slowed resolution and harmed satisfaction.

    Approach
    Built a real-time expert system using predictive modeling and economic logic. Deployed in SAS on mainframe for seamless call-center integration.

    Impact
    Reduced resolution time by 90%, cut costs by 30%, and increased customer satisfaction across the board.

    💬 Early NLP for Customer Service Prioritization

    Client Context
    Rep notes contained valuable urgency cues but lacked scalable processing.

    Approach
    Used keyword extraction and sentiment rules to create escalation indicators. Embedded into triage models.

    Impact
    Enabled targeted routing of priority cases. Proved the value of unstructured data in service ops.

    💡 Lifetime Value Modeling for Service Differentiation

    Client Context
    Static segmentation limited proactive service across customers and merchants.

    Approach
    Built predictive LTV and suppression risk models using transaction and cost data. Deployed for batch-based prioritization.

    Impact
    Improved identification of high-value accounts and reduced churn by 6%. Lowered servicing costs by 7%.

    📬 Optimizing Lead Generation for Financial Advisors

    Client Context
    Over-reliance on massive mail campaigns strained budgets and ROI.

    Approach
    Modeled response/conversion for RFI/RFC leads and ranked prospects by efficiency score.

    Impact
    Met lead demand at half the original volume. Improved campaign cost-efficiency and advisor satisfaction.

    🧪 Accelerating Marketing Insights with Multivariable Testing

    Client Context
    Legacy testing lacked nuance and masked variable interactions.

    Approach
    Used Plackett-Burman followed by Resolution IV factorial designs to uncover main and interaction effects.

    Impact
    Streamlined testing and uncovered high-impact creatives. Established a scalable testing standard.

    🎯 Advisor Focus List for Targeted Engagement

    Client Context
    Scaling outreach demanded a more intelligent approach to advisor prioritization.

    Approach
    Used LightGBM to model sales propensity. Created the Advisor Focus List with dual-level segmentation and evaluation via score matching.

    Impact
    Focus advisors produced 40% more sales. Became cornerstone of wholesaler strategy.

    📈 Modeling Optimal Contact Strategy Using LSTM

    Client Context
    Leadership suspected contact sequence optimization could improve sales.

    Approach
    Used LSTM to model time-dependent advisor responses. Included contact type and cadence features.

    Impact
    Identified 5–8 contact sweet spot and flagged suboptimal sequences. Refined wholesaler outreach strategy.

    🔄 Quantifying Advisor Response to Investment Recommendations

    Client Context
    Research group sought behavioral validation for quarterly signals.

    Approach
    Built time-series models aligning advisor trades/net flows to rec timelines. Modeled decay effects.

    Impact
    Buy recommendations increased trading volume by 50%, sell by 30%. Validated research ROI and influence.

    🧬 Validating Retirement Recommendations with Synthetic Data

    Client Context
    Retiree data lacked diversity for stress-testing proprietary models.

    Approach
    Used SMOTE and noise-injected sampling to generate 80M synthetic profiles. Ran benchmark and proprietary models across population.

    Impact
    Confirmed model alignment with fiduciary goals. Strengthened go-to-market confidence and model fairness.
